The story behind a great family printer in Peru: EDDILI S.A.C.

Our most sincere greetings through this communication, we want to let you know that we provide references and information of our represented: Editorial y Distribuidora de Libros S.A.C. A family business with a history of more than 35 years in the graphic market, dedicated to providing support to educational entities, industrial companies and commerce in general. Counting with suitable machinery for the design, edition, printing of various materials (paper and cardboard) in flat format 50 x 70 cm. Production of magazines, books, posters, food labels, bags and packages with varnish and plasticized finishes. A flexographic printing area for reels in width greater than 1.30 mt., Very useful for our national market.

We have a plant located on the main avenue with good capacity for receiving, producing and storing materials in reams and reels with security cameras and private surveillance. Our goal: We are committed to supporting your company to guarantee the development of your projects, always being at your service.

"The company's success is based on 10% inspiration and 90% work."
